Some random information
Morphology is the study of morphemes, which are the smallest units in a language that carry meanings.
Phonetics is the study of the acoustic and articulatory properties of speech sounds.
Syntax is the study of sentence structure and the relations between elements in a sentence, such as words, features, morphemes, etc. This is my main expertise.
Semantics is the study of meaning and concepts. In some way, it is the most abstract subfield in linguistics.
